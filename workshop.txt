description=S4 Economy is a comprehensive multiplayer economy overhaul for Project Zomboid (B42). It transforms the survival experience by introducing a multi-layered financial system, digital connectivity, and corporate logistics. This isn't just a shop; it's a new way to live—and die—in Knox Country.

description=? Overview
description=S4 Economy brings a realistic digital infrastructure to the apocalypse. Manage your wealth through a secure banking system, trade via a deep-web interface, and set up complex delivery networks to survive the Kentucky nightmare.
description=
description=?? Required Hardware
description=To fully access the S4 network, you will need:
description=
description=Computer: Your gateway to the system. Most functions are accessed here.
description=Credit Card: Your digital wallet. Register it at an ATM to store and transfer funds.
description=Power Bar (Multi-outlet): Essential to power your workstation (requires electricity).
description=Satellite Antenna: Must be placed outside to provide internet connectivity.
description=Card Reader: A mod-specific attachment for your computer to read your credit card.
description=Signal Device: Used to set delivery locations for your purchases.
description=? Setup Guide: Step-by-Step
description=1. Financial Foundation Find an ATM to register your first credit card. Set it as your Main Account to ensure all sales and rewards are funneled correctly.
description=
description=2. The Workstation Place your Computer and install a Power Bar. Ensure you have a working electricity source (Generator or Grid).
description=
description=3. Digital Uplink Place a Satellite Antenna outdoors. Use Electric Wire to connect the antenna to your computer.
description=
description=4. Establishing Connectivity Turn on the computer and open Internet Explorer. Navigate to the Survivor Network homepage.
description=
description=Note: You can find the Card Reader recipe here.
description=Purchase a Network Contract (Day/Week/Month/Year) to unlock the internet.
description=5. Preparing for Logistics Once online, visit the Good Shop homepage. Here you can find the Signal Device recipe.
description=
description=6. Full Integration Manufacture the Card Reader and Signal Device at a workbench. Install them onto your computer.
description=
description=7. Ready to Trade Insert your registered card into the computer. You are now ready to buy supplies or sell your hard-earned loot! Use the Signal Device on the ground to mark where you want your deliveries to land.
description=
description=? Admin: Managing the Shop
description=Debug Mode: In Singleplayer, entering debug mode unlocks the GoodShop Admin icon on the computer.
description=Customization: The shop starts empty. Register items, set prices, and stock levels manually.
description=Exporting: Configurations are saved at C:\Users\<Name>\Zomboid\Lua\S4Economy. It is recommended to create an add-on mod with these exports for dedicated servers.
