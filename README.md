**1. Financial Foundation**
Find an **ATM** to register your first credit card. Set it as your **Main Account** to ensure all sales and rewards are funneled correctly.

**2. The Workstation**
Place your **Computer** and install a **Power Bar**. Ensure you have a working electricity source (Generator or Grid).

**3. Digital Uplink**
Place a **Satellite Antenna** outdoors. Use **Electric Wire** to connect the antenna to your computer.

**4. Establishing Connectivity**
Turn on the computer and open **Internet Explorer**. Navigate to the **Survivor Network** homepage. 
*   *Note:* You can find the **Card Reader recipe** here.
*   Purchase a Network Contract (Day/Week/Month/Year) to unlock the internet.

**5. Preparing for Logistics**
Once online, visit the **Good Shop** homepage. Here you can find the **Signal Device recipe**.

**6. Full Integration**
Manufacture the **Card Reader** and **Signal Device** at a workbench. Install them onto your computer. 

**7. Ready to Trade**
Insert your registered card into the computer. You are now ready to buy supplies or sell your hard-earned loot! Use the **Signal Device** on the ground to mark where you want your deliveries to land.
